‘We travel not to escape life but for life not to escape us.’

Sheina Raskin / YouTube
It began as a reward for finishing high school, the motivation was a year abroad. It was the first real taste of real freedom, real humility, real beauty. After returning home to university two years later, every holiday was a chance to travel again. The wanderlust in her only grew with every trip and in 2016 she laughed the travel wear brand Sheina. Sheina represents the holiday lifestyle as real life rather then a get way. As the founder and designer, Sheina, embodying the muse, curated a minimal travel wardrobe as a response to the stresses of packing. Through the use of performance fabric, layering and capsule dressing, Sheina allows the modern traveller to pack less and travel more. Stay forever curious xx
